About Liz

Hi, I’m Liz! FlavorsWithLiz started in my kitchen with a love for flavor and a passion for sharing it. I believe the best treats come from the heart — made with real ingredients, a bit of creativity, and plenty of love. From smooth fruit butters to fresh baked cookies, my goal is to make every bite feel warm, comforting, and full of flavor.

  • Locally sourced fruit when in season
  • No artificial colors or preservatives only personality
  • Packed and shipped with care from Monterey Bay
