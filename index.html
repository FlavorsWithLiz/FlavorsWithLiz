About Liz

Hi, I’m Liz — a self-taught chef and small-batch maker who believes dessert should be joyful and jams should taste like the fruit they came from. Every jar and cookie is crafted in tiny batches so flavor stays bright, textures stay perfect, and you can feel the care that went into it.

  • Locally sourced fruit when in season
  • No artificial colors or preservatives
  • Packed and shipped with care from Monterey Bay
