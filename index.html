FAQ

A few quick answers to common questions. If you’re still wondering about something, feel free to reach out!

Where are your treats made?

All fruit butters and cookies are made in a home kitchen in the Monterey Bay area. I follow safe food-handling practices and prep in small batches so everything tastes fresh and homemade.

Do your products contain allergens?

Yes, many items contain common allergens such as wheat, dairy, eggs, and gluten. While I do my best to avoid cross-contact, everything is made in the same home kitchen, so I cannot guarantee an allergen-free environment. Please share any allergies in your order form notes.

What is your refund or cancellation policy?

Because everything is made fresh to order, all sales are final once payment is received. If you need to cancel or adjust your order, please contact me as soon as possible before payment or within the timeframe noted in your confirmation email. I’m always happy to work with you when I can.

How long do the fruit butters and cookies stay fresh?

Fruit butters keep best when stored in the refrigerator and enjoyed within 2-3 weeks after opening. Cookies are best within 2–3 days of pickup or delivery for peak freshness, but you can freeze them in an airtight container to enjoy later.

Do you offer pickup, shipping, or both?

I currently offer local pickup in the Monterey Bay area and shipping within the U.S. You can choose your preference in the order form. Pickup details and shipping timing will be confirmed by email.

How far in advance should I place my order?

For most orders, up to 5 days’ notice is perfect. For larger or event orders, I recommend reaching out at least 2 weeks in advance so I can plan ingredients and baking time.

Do you take custom or special-event orders?

Yes! I love working on gifts, party favors, office thank-yous, and seasonal specials. Use the notes section on the order form or email me directly, and we can chat about flavors, quantities, and timing.

How do I actually pay?

After you submit the order form, I’ll email you an order summary and total. Payment options typically include Zelle, PayPal, and cash for local pickup. Once payment is received, I’ll begin preparing your order.

Delicious ways to enjoy fruit butters

Fruit butters are more than just a spread — they’re a little jar of flavor you can use all over your kitchen.

  • Spread on warm toast, English muffins, or bagels.
  • Swirl into oatmeal, overnight oats, or yogurt bowls.
  • Spoon over waffles, pancakes, or French toast.
  • Pair with cheese boards (they’re lovely with brie, goat cheese, or sharp cheddar).
  • Use as a glaze for chicken or pork for a sweet-savory touch.
  • Bake into muffins, cakes, turnovers, or sandwich cookies.
  • Add to charcuterie boards as a bright, fruity accent.
